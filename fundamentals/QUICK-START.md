## Common Questions

### "What if I get negative comments?"

**Answer:** Direct communication is one of your values. Engage honestly:
- "What's worked better for you?" (learn from them)
- "Happy to discuss â€” DM me and we can dig into specifics."

### "What if nobody engages?"

**First week is slow.** Keep shipping. Engagement compounds.

**Tactics to boost:**
- Comment on 10 posts/day from your target audience (CTOs, VPs of Eng)
- Tag people in your posts ("Shoutout to @person for teaching me this")
- Cross-post to Reddit (r/startups, r/saas, r/engineering)

### "Do I need professional graphics?"

**No.** Authenticity > polish for the first 30 days.

Use Canva free templates. Or pay $20 on Fiverr for 4 carousels.

Your ideas matter more than your design skills.

### "What if someone steals my content?"

**Good.** It means it's valuable.

Your IP is **how you implement these systems**, not the ideas themselves. Anyone can read about two-way doors. You're the one who installs them.
